Tina Beatty-Smith

A tester… on a journey to become a dev team member.

So...a little about me.

I am a Daughter. Wife. Mother. Friend. Tester … but my passion is quality. Not quality in the sense of reviewing documents for spelling and syntax errors or checking for correct signatures. While this aspect of quality is important, especially in a regulated environment, my focus is on "did we deliver what the client wanted". In my experience as a manual software tester, my expertise was not utilized until late in the development process- regardless of the development methodology. This and my quality focus have led me to begin the journey to become a more valuable member of the development team. The QA position necessitated exploring the code and asking the how, what, why questions. Yet the knowledge gained was not enough to bridge the gap to be a team member that could add meaningful value in the early stages of development. When the opportunity to formally learn the technical skills necessary to bridge that gap became available, I jumped with both feet in. My journey is long and winding, sometimes cloudy, and even stormy; but I continue to learn and utilize new skills knowing this journey will result in me being that value member of the development team.
